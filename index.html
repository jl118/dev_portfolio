About Me

Hi! I’m Jennifer, a Full-Stack Web Developer with a Bachelor’s Degree in Communication from UNC Chapel Hill positioned to provide unique perspectives on how end users interact with websites and software platforms by leveraging my background in media technology and digital production.

I’ve always had an interest in puzzles, art, and storytelling, so after being awarded a spot in UNC's Hollywood Internship Program, I started my career in digital and film production shortly after graduation. Though I was able to collaborate with amazing people in this field and gain invaluable experience with creative problem resolution, interpersonal and mass communication, and leading teams to accomplish goals in a fast-paced environment, I am most thankful for the fact that it inadvertently introduced me to my true passion: web development.

Pursuing this passion, I am in the process of earning a certificate in Full Stack Web Development through UNC Chapel Hill, where I can expand my skills in JavaScript, CSS, MySQL, Node, and responsive web design. I am interested in using technology to solve problems efficiently, while striving to create a more engaging and intuitive user experience. I’m seeking a role as a web developer at a company where I can learn, grow, and continue to master the craft, and am excited to utilize my skills in communication as part of a quality-driven team to build better experiences on the web.
